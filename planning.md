# Project 3: TakeMeter — Planning

## Community
r/AmITheAsshole (r/AITA)

## Label Taxonomy

`interpersonal` — The conflict is between the poster and someone they have an
ongoing relationship with (family, romantic partner, friends, or coworkers).
The key signal is that the poster knows this person by name or role.

`stranger_conflict` — The conflict involves someone the poster has no prior
relationship with (a customer, a stranger in public, a new neighbor, etc.).
The key signal is that the poster has no ongoing relationship with the other party.

## Decision Rule
If the post provides specific evidence that the poster knows the other person
(calls them "my mom", "my boyfriend", "my coworker"), label it `interpersonal`.
If the other party is anonymous or incidental ("a guy at the store", "some
customer"), label it `stranger_conflict`. For ambiguous cases — like a neighbor
the poster just met once — label it `stranger_conflict` since no real
relationship exists yet.

## Hard Case Example
A conflict with a neighbor:
- Lived next door for years and interact regularly → `interpersonal`
- Just moved in last week → `stranger_conflict`
